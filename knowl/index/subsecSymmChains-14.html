The chains in Figure 4.5.2 are laid out as in the recursive construction. And then, in Figure 4.5.3, additional thin edges are added, each of which corresponds to a cover relation in the Hasse diagram of the lattice; that is, a change of a 0 to a 1 when moving upward along that relation. Each relation, including those of the SCD, are labelled according to the index of the bit that changes. The added edges form a pair of trees, one of which is a reflection of the other. It it worth noting that the rightmost elements of the chains appear in colex order. The upper tree can be formed, in general, by obtaining the parent of an element by changing its leftmost 0 into a 1. Call the resulting diagram \(D\text{;}\) we will show that it is the dual of a Venn diagram.
