Definition 4.5.1.

The Boolean lattice, \(B_n\text{,}\) is the partial order whose elements are the subsets of \([n]\text{,}\) ordered by inclusion. A chain in \(B_n\) is a sequence \(S_1 \subseteq S_2 \subseteq \cdots \subseteq S_t\) of elements of \(B_n\) such that \(|S_i|=|S_{i-1}|+1\text{.}\) The chain is symmetric if \(|S_1|+|S_t| = n\text{.}\) A symmetric chain decomposition (or SCD) of \(B_n\) is a partition of the elements of \(B_n\) into symmetric chains.
