There is a much richer set of symmetries of the sphere than there on the plane, and it is natural to investigate whether these additional symmetries can be possessed by Venn diagrams. As the reader works their way through this subsection, it can be useful to have actual spheres to hold and view. The author once bought a gross of white ping pong balls to draw on while investigating spherical Venn diagrams with his grad students!
