Note that the Hasse diagram of \(B_n\text{,}\) regarded as a graph, is isomorphic to \(Q_n\text{.}\) It is a well-known result of order theory that \(B_n\) has a SCD for every natural number \(n\text{,}\) and that the number of chains in the SCD is \({n \choose \lfloor n/2 \rfloor}\text{.}\) Regard each of the elements of \(B_n\) as a bitstring of length \(n\text{.}\) We will outline two methods of obtaining a symmetric chain decomposition.
